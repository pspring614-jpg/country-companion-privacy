Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 18, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Country Companion. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using Country Companion, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

Information We Collect

Country Companion is designed to work primarily on your device without requiring an account or transmitting personal data to external servers. Information stored locally on your device includes:

  • Calendar tasks, notes, and activity records you create within the app
  • Property zone details and supply lists you enter
  • App settings and preferences

This data is stored exclusively on your device using local storage and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party unless you explicitly choose to share it.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials (no login is required)
  • Your precise GPS location
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Any financial or payment information

AI Guide Feature

The AI Guide screen provides homesteading advice based on a built-in local knowledge base. Conversations within the AI Guide are processed on-device and are not transmitted to external AI services or stored beyond your current session.

Third-Party Services

Country Companion does not currently integrate third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social media platforms. If this changes in a future update, this policy will be updated accordingly and you will be notified through the app.

Data Storage and Security

All data you enter into Country Companion is stored locally on your device. We recommend keeping your device protected with a passcode or biometric lock to safeguard your information. We do not have access to data stored on your device and cannot recover it if your device is lost or reset.

Children's Privacy

Country Companion is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Your Rights

Because Country Companion stores all data locally on your device, you have full control over your information at all times. You may delete any data you have entered directly within the app, or by uninstalling the app from your device, which will remove all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the Effective Date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of Country Companion after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
